About the App

Virtual Visit provides a collection of social stories about going to the doctor in order to help people with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) prepare for their doctor's appointments.


Social stories detail each step of a situation, which eases anxiety and sets expectations about what will happen.


The social stories in Virtual Visit are about:

  • Getting to a specific doctor's office on the campus of Boston Medical Center (BMC)
  • Common medical procedures:
    • Measuring height and weight
    • Measuring blood pressure
    • Getting a shot
    • Getting blood drawn
    • Physical exam
    • Dentist appointment

For questions or comments, please email autismprogram@bmc.org.
